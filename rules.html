Welcome to the Simon Game!

The rules are as follows:

  • Once started, the game will start flashing the colours that need to be clicked.
  • Upon clicking the correct colour, the player moves onto the next level.
  • As the player keeps moving up the levels, the sequence of the colours becomes longer.
  • The player must remember the correct sequence order and click on the colours accordingly.
  • Clicking on the wrong colour will lead to the game ending.
